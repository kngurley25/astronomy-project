Welcome to Mars!

Explore the Landscape

Mars is the home to five rovers. The names of these brave troopers are Sojourner, Spirit, Opportunity, Curiosity, and Perseverance.
Spirit and Opportunity landed on Mars in 2004 in search for signs of past life, where they were dropped on opposite sides of the red planet. They make atmospheric observations and field geography.
Curiosity started their journey in 2011 and has the same purpose as their rover colleagues. Curiosity's mission has been extended indefinitely, exploring the now familiar Mars.

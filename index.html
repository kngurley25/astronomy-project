Test Your Knowledge of Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and the next planet beyond Earth. On average it is more than 142,000,000 miles from the sun.

Mars generally has a warm climate, with average temperatures well above 100 degrees Fahrenheit or 37 degrees Celsius.

Unlike Earth, Mars does not currently have any moons in orbit around it.
